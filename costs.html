CPM

Definition: Cost per Thousand(CPM), Advertisers pay when their messages are exposed to specific audiences. For every 1,000 advertisement impressions on one webpage the advertiser must pay. EX: $10 CPM means every 1,000 impressions of its ad they must pay $10.


Click here for more information on CPM.

CPC

Definition: Cost per Click (CPC), Advertisers pay everytime a user clicks on their ads.

CPA

Definition: Cost per Action (CPA), Advertisers only pay when a specific action (generally a purchase) is performed.
